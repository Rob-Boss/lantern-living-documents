### 🌟 Debra Spark (Family Friend & Author)
*   **Role & Bio:** Author and magazine contributing author who has written for *Dwell*, *Decor Maine*, *Maine Home + Design*, *Yankee*, the *New York Times*, and others.
*   **Warm Introductions:** Debra has offered to provide free, warm introductions to her direct contacts at **Decor Maine**, **Down East**, and **Maine Home + Design**.
*   **Feedback & Strategy:** 
    *   *PR Firm vs. Direct Pitches:* Editors do not favor pitches from PR firms over property owners, architects, or independent writers. She recommends avoiding overly packaged PR agency pitches.
    *   *Inverting the Process:* Do not write articles first and pitch them; editors prefer to define their own framing and word counts. Pitch the story concept instead.
