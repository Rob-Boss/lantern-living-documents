## 💡 Technical Optimization Guidelines
*   **The "See More" Hook:** Keep the most compelling hook in the first **125 characters** of the Primary Text so it is visible before mobile screens truncate it.
*   **Headline Length:** Keep headlines under **40 characters** to prevent truncation in the desktop/mobile feed formats.
*   **CTA Button:** Pair these ads with the **"Book Now"** call-to-action button.
