## 📝 Detailed Call Notes

### 1. [Cinch PR](https://www.cinchpr.com)
* **Date of Call:** June 3, 2026
* **Financials:** 
  * **Original Project Fee:** $25,000 minimum total for a 3-month summer launch.
  * **Updated Fee (June 10):** Offered to match UpSpring's pricing at $6,500/month for a 4-month minimum ($26,000 total) to stay competitive.
  * **Expenses:** $200/month administrative fees + travel. Prefer sending 2 people for a site visit ($2,000–$2,500), up to $5,000 total travel if needed. Can be done remotely if required to save cost.
* **Scope & Logistics:**
  * **Strategy:** Laser-focused regional outreach targeting the Northeast corridor/feeder markets. Focus on short-term digital coverage to trickle into fall. (Unlikely to secure major national placements like *The New York Times* or *Condé Nast Traveler* in this short window).
  * **Assets Needed:** High demand for brand assets upfront (Q&A, digital assets, influencer briefs). Need to identify a key spokesperson and clarify growth stories (e.g., "Is this the first of many camps?").
  * **Influencer Relations:** Recommends allocating an additional $10,000 influencer budget. Influencers provide more immediate coverage.
  * **Friction Points:** With a smaller fee like this, they will share a curated list of influencers/contacts, but the client must handle negotiation and execution.
* **Our Take:** High capability. Followed up on June 10 to match UpSpring's pricing of $6,500/month for 4 months. Because they are a smaller boutique outfit, we are keeping them close as a strong backup option in case negotiations with UpSpring fall through.
